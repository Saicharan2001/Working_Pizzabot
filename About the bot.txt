
The Pizza bot is created using the google cloud platform called Dialogflow which is a natural language understanding platform which enables us to design and integrate a conversational user interface which can be dedicated to any services , into our applications seamlessly

Services offered by this bot :

1. General conversation with the Customer

2. Displays the menu card on your request and this contains the whole list of available pizzas

3. Takes up your order

4. Requests for any toppings needed

5. After the order is successfully taken from the customer , then it takes up your contact details like phone number and address to deliver the pizza 

6. Also considers any additional instructions ( e.g., speeding up the delivery process )
